30 day challenge

March 12, 2025

Surfing used to take up such a big part of my life. after a sudden injury, not only surfing but even the things i loved, exercising, or simple daily tasks like grocery shopping became difficult. There were many days when i felt helpless and stayed in bed for hours. So i started this 30-day challenge as a way to plant a small seed of motivation in myself every day. No dramatic change after the 30-day challenge. Life just went back to normal.  I still want to paint but starting again feels hard. That daily pressure, finishing one piece every 24 hours, actually helped more than I thought. I even thought about doing 100 days, but instead I feel drawn to something bigger. So my next challenge would be a large painting. Maybe one day, my work will hang on a white wall like one at the local gallery.
